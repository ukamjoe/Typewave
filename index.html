Every transcript, exactly as it happened

Four things that separate a usable transcript from a wall of guessed text.

Timestamps that click

Every AI segment can carry a timestamp. Click one in the transcript and the audio jumps straight there.

[00:14:22] The witness then described the vehicle as
[00:14:31] dark blue, possibly a sedan.
◐

Speakers, kept straight

Tag Speaker A and B as the conversation moves, so the transcript reads like the conversation actually sounded.

SPK-A: So when did you first notice the issue?
SPK-B: About three weeks before the audit.
⚑

Flags for what's unclear

One tap marks crosstalk, pauses, or inaudible moments — so nothing gets quietly invented to fill a gap.

He said the shipment would arrive [unclear]
before [crosstalk] the end of the month.
